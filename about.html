About me

My Story

Hi, I’m Savannah! I live in Somerset, Wisconsin and work in White Bear Lake Township, Minnesota. My education and my work life has focused on animal care and environmental education. I love to bring people outside and making sure they have a enjoyable experience in the outdoors. Learning is so important to me as well as making sure I can share my knowledge with people in thoughtful and meaningful ways. I have working in both the non-profit sector and the government sector. With my experience in these sectors I’ve found a lacking in user friendly resources and wanted to get into the UX design field to see what I can do make environment and wildlife resources and tools more accessible.

My journey in UX design has be a huge undertaken and has been an rewarding experience. I have grown in my research, interview, and analysis skills. I have learned how to use tools like Figma. I am eager to continue to improve technical skills and utilize my interpersonal skills to help users.

With my free time I love to go hiking, birding, kayaking, puzzling, traveling, play games, and hanging out with friends and family.

I am excited to continue to grow my skills, help users, and share my creative!
